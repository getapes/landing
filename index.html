The Opportunity

Commercial airspace has
no operating system. Yet.

In 2018, two drones shut down Gatwick Airport for 36 hours. Over 1,000 flights grounded. £50M+ in losses. Nobody could stop them. That was seven years ago — and drones have only become cheaper, faster, and harder to detect.

Today, the market offers fragments. Sensor vendors detect but don't decide. EW/ECM providers jam narrowly — and autonomous drones fly without radio links. Defence contractors sell million-dollar systems behind classified procurement cycles. The commercial world has no end-to-end answer.

⚠

Operational Downtime

One drone sighting can halt a production line for hours. Airports, ports, and logistics hubs lose millions per incident.

👁

Espionage & Surveillance

A camera drone over your facility for 12 minutes captures layouts, processes, and personnel movements. You may never know it happened.

☠

Safety & Liability

A drone collision with personnel or equipment creates injury, insurance claims, and regulatory scrutiny — with the operator held accountable for inaction.

📈

Reputational Damage

A publicized airspace breach signals to clients, insurers, and regulators that your security posture has a visible gap.

Platform Architecture

Sense. Decide. Act.

Three layers. One continuous audit backbone. Every sensor consumed, every decision traced, every action compliant — designed for global scale from day one.

Layer 01

Sense

Perception

Ingest data from any sensor — RF, optical, thermal, radar, acoustic — and normalize it into a unified stream. We don't make sensors. We consume them all.

Sensor-agnostic abstraction layer

Modular — add, swap, replace freely

Decide

Intelligence

Classify objects by type, behaviour, and intent. Evaluate against jurisdiction-specific policies. Determine the right response. This is the core IP.
